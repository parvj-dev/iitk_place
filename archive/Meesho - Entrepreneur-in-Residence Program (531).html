Job Description : 
The single biggest reason why Meesho has become the fastest growing startup in India is because we 
are a team of entrepreneurs. 
More than 70% of our team at the top and mid level have either started their own companies in the 
past, or been an early employee at a startup. True entrepreneurs are hungry to succeed, willing to 
own big challenges and aren’t shy of wearing multiple hats : all being traits that we highly value in the 
company. 
Unlike most companies that are a copycat of western models, Meesho is solving a unique problem by 
building a massive wave of micro-entrepreneurs across the world. As you read this - there’s a decent 
chance that your mom is sitting at home and using the Meesho app to run her own business. The kind 
of social impact this creates is inspiring and often gives us goosebumps - http://bit.ly/2NrjZYx. 
Building for this unique market requires us to discard conventional wisdom, and apply first-principles 
thinking whenever possible. Through the EIR program, Meesho wants to create the perfect platform 
for young graduates to apply this first-principles approach to some of the biggest problems in our 
fast-growing market. 
We also recognise that most young graduates don’t have a clear picture on whether they want to 
pursue a pure product or pure business/growth role in a startup. Hence, we have broken the EIR 
program down into Product & Business Tracks : giving you the flexibility to explore both. As you grow 
in this program, you will be working on problems usually reserved for senior managers. 
If you have strong interest levels in one of these tracks - we would like you to pick one, but otherwise 
- an EIR will be exploring both tracks one after the other, working 1-on-1 with some of the smartest 
Product Managers & Senior Business Leaders in the company. 
If this is something that excites you, we can’t wait to hear from you. You can read more about our 
culture here - http://bit.ly/34Bq0r5.
