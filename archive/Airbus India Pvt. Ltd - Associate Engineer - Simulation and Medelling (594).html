Designation : Associate Engineer - Simulation and Medelling
Description :

Activities: 

The job holder is responsible for delivering simulation models on Time, Cost and Quality. 
The various activities to be performed are: 
    Model development and validation across different aircraft programs: 
    Carry out analysis of the requirements from the design office, testing teams and the integration teams 
    Develop new models or implement updates to existing models and synthesize simulation results 
    Develop new prototypes to support design office studies 
    Ensure functional validation and appropriate level of representatives required 
    Provide support to the design office, integration and testing teams to enable efficient use of the simulations 
    Interfacing with relevant counterparts in Europe to ensure maximum effectiveness of model development 

Required skills and expertise: 
    Knowledge of aircraft architecture, basic a/c behavior, and systems 
    Experience in C, C++ programming 
    Knowledge and experience in simulation and modelling (Matlab, Simulink, SCADE, etc.) 
    Working knowledge of Configuration Management (SVN, CVS, etc.) 
    Strong inter-personal and communication skills 
    Flexibility and adaptability
