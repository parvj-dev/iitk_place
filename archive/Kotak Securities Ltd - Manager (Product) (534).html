Key Responsibilities and Accountabilities:

 

  1. Understand the market and customer requirements
  2. Do in depth research to come up with information needed to come up with the new product
  3. Do regular competition tracking to understand the market place for the product and also expected penetration
  4. Do complete analysis if in case product may lead to cannibalization of revenue from existing customers than time taken to break even along with details of new acquisition required from the product
  5. Develop product sales strategies to get the desired market share from the product
  6. Periodic update on metrics to management for measurement of success of the product
  7. Owning complete execution of the product, keeping all stakeholders in loop and ensure timely launch
  8. Come up with newer product or enhance existing product features which can act differentiator in market for acquiring good quality customers and enhance ticket size of existing customers
  9. Regular training to customer touch points on the products managed
  10. Regularly self-update to gain sufficient knowledge required by different means
  11. Thinking the apt way to market the product to end consumers and work closely with marketing team for successful launch and regular business from the product

 

Skills & Knowledge

  1. Out of the box approach with strategic thinking
