Job Description & Skills Required

 

Gaana is looking for Business Analyst who will be working on developing strategic models to generate meaningful insights to facilitate data driven decision making across multiple business teams such as Product, Marketing.

 

  1. Understanding drivers, impacts, and key influences on business P&L dynamics.
  2. Partnering with the multiple teams to create key performance indicators and new methodologies for measurement.
  3. Develop and standardize processes for benchmarking performance and generate insights for marketing and product teams
  4. Translating data into actionable insights for the stakeholders.
  5. Automate reporting for weekly business metrics, identify areas of opportunity to automate and scale ad-hoc analyses.
  6. Develop analytical models to evaluate customer lifetime value, cohort analysis, user segmentation among others
  7. Thinks critically and creates analyses to identify key priorities for new country launches

 

Required skills and qualification:

  1. Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, IT, or Mathematics related field
  2. Ability to impact operations and effect change
  3. Detail oriented, analytical and inquisitive
  4. Ability to work independently and with others
  5. Extremely organized with strong time-management skills
  6. Big Picture Thinking
  7. Problem Solving
  8. Communication and Relationship Building
