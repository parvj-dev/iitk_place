Role Title

Manufacturing Test & Tools

Grade

MTT Engineer

What the role entails

(Key Responsibilities) 
    • Expertise with power electronics
    • Extensive experience with rack and stack test platforms
    • Knowledge of battery chemistry and technology is a plus

What we are looking for in you

(Skills, Knowledge, Special Attributes, Mobility)
  • Design verification – Confirm that a new product design meets requirements
  • Compliance – Certify that our products meet compliance standards
  • Reliability – Warrant product is reliable over its lifetime
  • System verification – Validate that Enphase products interoperate as a system
  • Understanding the basic technology, understanding the needs of each of the groups and coordinate development of a test framework that can be used for all these functions with a maximum amount of reuse
  • Fulfilling vastly different goals of the Enphase manufacturing test hardware and software to be deployed at Enphase’s various production facilities.
  • Drive results in unique product features, disciplined manufacturing processes, and rigorous testing
  • Working with geographically dispersed teams consisting of Electronics Hardware Engineers, Software Engineers and Engineering Technicians
  • Evaluating Marketing and Engineering Requirement Documents to develop robust Test Requirements that drive reliability while reducing the cost of test
  • Evaluating applicable standards for each product and designing test platforms to implement required testing
  • Developing and documenting test system design and maintenance routines, including calibration, replacement schedule, repeatability test, etc.
  • Coordinating closely with the Mfg. SW Development Team to design and ensure a fault-tolerant test system
  • Maintaining, debugging and supporting the production facilities where the test systems are deployed
  • Driving closed loop response to factory and field failures (RMA) by identifying test gaps and opportunities
