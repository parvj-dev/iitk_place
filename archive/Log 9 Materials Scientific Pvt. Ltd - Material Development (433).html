Position Summary

Log 9 Materials is seeking a talented and passionate candidate for the position of research fellow. The candidate will be responsible for developing research objectives, projects and proposals, undertaking research and evaluating the outcomes. He/she will be responsible for compiling and analyzing and presenting the results to concerned senior management.

 

Responsibilities

  • Perform theoretical literature reviews for the research projects
  • Plan and execute laboratory research to prove feasibility of concepts
  • Conduct individual and/or collaborative research experiments
  • Evaluate and present outcomes of the research
  • Apply scientific principles for continuous betterment of the products
  • Preparing reports and presentations related to the research work
  • Bring new ideas to solve research problems, strategize the research plan of action
  • Coordinate and work efficiently with other team members
  • Other duties and/or responsibilities may be assigned to researchers by management

 

Qualifications

B. Tech./ M. Tech. in Material Science or Nanotechnology or Chemical Sciences or related fields

 

Desired Skills and Knowledge

  • Industrial or academic internships in related field
  • Excellent understanding of contemporary research methodologies
  • Hands on experience on wet chemistry processes
  • Experience of synthesizing and characterizing various nano materials
  • Knowledge of principles and operating methods of various laboratory equipment
  • Ability to work efficiently with minimal supervision
  • Ability to work independently as well as within a team
