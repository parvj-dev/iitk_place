Job Description

Provide Experience Design expertise for projects that require User Research, Usability Evaluation, Information Architecture, Interaction and Visual Design to ensure quality of software solutions from an Experience Design standpoint.

Responsibilities:

As a Lead – Experience Design, you will be an expert contributing to different phases of the Design lifecycle, consulting and delivering UX Solutions to our esteemed clients.

 

 

  • You will be intensely involved in requirements gathering (user-centric), translating requirements into relevant UX activities and deliverables including epic/user story/user flow creation and documentation, sketches, wireframes, journey map design, or higher-fidelity visual designs
  • You will be proficient in managing client / business stakeholder expectations, work harmoniously with other stakeholders of the project from a Delivery perspective, guide development teams on design process, guidelines, deliverables; contribute to the proposal development, scope definition, mentoring and internal capability building
  • You will have the opportunity to shape value-adding Experience Design consulting solutions that enable our clients to meet the changing needs of the global landscape. You will be required to facilitate design workshops and engage with stakeholders and end users
  • You will be required to create high Level design brief containing - objective/goal, resources, team structure, target audience, task/activity and milestone projections.
  • You will be proficient with conducting task analysis, define the information architecture, concept and detailed design, prototyping and deliver specifications for the consumption of the front end development teams
  • You will be required to create navigation model/page flows/site taxonomy and interaction design
  • You will be required to create low fidelity wireframes to illustrate and evaluate application design, content structure and navigation
  • You will be conversant with industry best practices (User Experience Design), usage of wire-framing, visual design and prototyping tools (In Design, Illustrator, Dreamweaver, Photoshop, Sketch, etc.)

Competencies

  • Strong analytical and communication skills 
  • Knows basic Design Principles like affordance, grouping, consistency, Gestalt principles, etc. in order to create screen designs
  • Breaks down the problem into its constituent parts to get a better understanding of issues. Evaluates the available solution options while solving problems. Ability to bring in a Design Thinking approach (philosophy) to solving business problems
  • Structures thoughts, uses simple words and is proficient in the English language, listens attentively and asks questions to seek clarity for oneself, communicates with conviction and is confident to share own point of view in team meetings
  • Ability to anchor usability review procedures and compile usability reports; Ability to independently interface with project teams and deliver UI design services for simple to medium level projects.  Ability to apply formal UI design methods such as User Profiling, Expert Reviews

The objective for a new joinee in the UX practice will be to assist, plan, manage and execute UI design delivery for simple-to-medium projects independently
