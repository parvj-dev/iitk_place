About Us
Solv is a ‘conversational commerce’ digital platform offering financial services, business solutions and
networking avenues for Small and Medium scale businesses.
Description
Solv is looking for a Data Scientist who will focus on development of Machine Learning (ML) solutions
across the customer lifecycle in order to facilitate enhanced decision making on the platform.
What You Will Do
• Build scorecards using ML algorithms (e.g. Random Forest, Gradient Boosting, XGBoost, Deep
Learning) related to Credit Risk, Fraud Risk, Chance of Approval, Portfolio Management, etc.
• Closely coordinate with other functions such as Product, Technology and Risk and align on
business and technical needs
• Identify optimization / automation opportunities in model development process such as
Documentation, Turnaround Time (TAT), etc.
• Liaise with Data and Systems teams to deploy ML solutions quickly and accurately
• Participate in strategic initiatives to build in-house analytics capabilities for the platform
Who You Are
• A ‘detail obsessed’ professional who puts customer experience at the heart of any product design
• A technology enthusiast who is up-to-date with the latest developments in the world of
technology
• Someone who can:
o Balance customer delight, technology viability and business feasibility
o Think big and take ownership of deliverables from ideation to execution
o Embrace chaos, uncertainty and volatility
o Work in a dynamic environment with a diverse set of stakeholders
What You Possess
• Expert command of statistical concepts such as Bayes Theorem, Central Limit Theorem, ANOVA,
Z and T Statistics, Hypothesis Testing etc.
• Expert command of SQL, R, Python, Scala, Java etc. as applied to data science.
• Hands-on experience in data analytics, e.g. Data Pre-Processing, Feature Engineering, PCA,
Model Development / Validation, Generalization / Regularization Techniques.
• Excellent understanding of machine learning techniques and algorithms such as Clustering,
Linear Regression, Logistic Regression, Time Series, KNN, Decision Tree, Random Forest,
XGBoost, SVM etc.
• Experience with data visualization tools such as Tableau, Power BI etc.
• Hands-on experience in Web scraping, NLP, RNN, CNN etc. is a plus.
• Ability to communicate the results of analysis to Business in a clear and effective manner.
• Strong analytical and logical skills with emphasis on relating ML insights with business intuition.
• Ability to take project ownership and innovate as and when required.
• Domain expertise in Risk Management and / or Credit Lending domain is preferred.
