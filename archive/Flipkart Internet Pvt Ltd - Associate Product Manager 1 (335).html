Associate Product Manager 1

The APM program is composed of an elite group of young technologists interested
in pushing the boundaries of commerce in India across a variety of spaces like payments, mobile, fulfillment and much more. The program is a two year rotational program that will expose APMs to all portions of the company and to the broad disciplines of business, marketing, product development, brand, and engineering.

APMs are given nearly irresponsible levels of responsibility at Flipkart with the intent of providing a fresh look at how things should be done while asking for great progress. APMs are responsible for shipping compelling software on a cross- functional team that delights millions of users everyday. The program is intended to serve as an alternative to a MBA, but MBA graduates with little or no work experience are encouraged to apply.
These young leaders are expected to move up in the ranks and push Flipkart to unimagined heights.

APM ROLE
Work with talented engineers, designers, product marketers, business executives and customers to imagine, design, build and launch things for real people.
Figure out where the world is headed and get there right in time (or a little early).
Help guide our flagship and newer products strategy and vision.

APM PROGRAM STRUCTURE

Two year rotation program comprised of two 1-year rotations in different product teams.
First rotation will be assigned, second rotation will be based on the candidate’s choice from amongst different product teams
Complemented with learning through business and design bootcamps, 1:1 coaching and mentoring by the topmost executives of the company

Upon graduation at the end of two years, APMs will become PMs in the product org.

WHY YOU SHOULD TAKE THIS ROLE
Join a top-10 privately valued company (worldwide) in an elite program to help 1B+ users in one of the world’s largest and most dynamic economies.
Become an expert builder and strategist for the mobile-only world: By 2020, there will be 600M Indians &lt; 24 years old who will likely have a smartphone.

Be responsible for core projects that propel Flipkart to new heights in Commerce, Payments, Fulfilment and many other adventures we pursue
Work alongside a world class product team composed of PMs and Engineers from Google, Ebay, Intel, Yahoo!, and other groundbreaking companies
There will be an application shortlist followed by a phone screen and at least 3 in- person interviews on campus.
