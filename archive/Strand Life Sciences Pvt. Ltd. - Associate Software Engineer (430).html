Company : Strand Life Sciences Pvt. Ltd.
Company Name : Strand Life Sciences Pvt. Ltd.
Nature Of Business : Bioinformatics Software development for clinical and research domains
Designation : Associate Software Engineer
Description :

Strand builds data mining and visualization platforms for biological data. A typical biological experiment to read the genome or transcriptome of a person could result in data as large as hundreds of gigabytes. Efficiently processing, visualizing and mining this data require careful software architecture and algorithms. The entry level software role will be to work as part of the core product development team to create specific applications to analyze and visualize this data.

  • Work will be mainly around research/design/development of software prototypes & products.
  • Relevant areas of expertise are Algorithms and Visualizations/ Statistical analysis/ Text mining/ Big Data/ Machine learning.
  • A person with good analytical skills having adequate systems knowledge and expert programming skills is preferred.

Programming languages: Java/ Python/ Javascript-HTML5-CSS.
