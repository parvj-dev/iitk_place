Description :

Jumio is the market leader in AI powered Identity verification. With >50 market share and expanding rapidly year on year the potential for professional growth and progression is limitless.

 

You’ll be working on a range of evolving products that help to eliminate identity fraud and make the digital world a safer place. With over 200M identities verified already and c200,000 transactions per day you can imagine the amount of data and the high levels of resilience, security and availability our technical teams get to play with. It’s high-scale, high-impact work with a positive purpose.

 

Digital identity is a core use case and 100s of top brands already trust Jumio to help them with customer on-boarding, KYC and AML across Financial Services, Sharing Economy, Retail and Gaming. The use cases are varied and evolving as are our products.

 

The new Bangalore engineering campus has Machine Learning Enablement as a core pillar as well as other projects with full end to end ownership of projects. This pillar is at the intersection of systems engineering, data engineering, and Machine Learning, with the goal being to support the Machine Learning workflows by making the data, toolset and process support available for data scientists and machine learning engineers. With established offices in Austria, Montreal, London, Palo Alto and Singapore – as well as our Operations centres in Jaipur, Udaipur and Colombia, there are lots of opportunities for travel and collaboration with the global teams.

 

We are looking to build on a culture of technical excellence and an environment where experimentation and continual learning are actively supported – including generous personal and team training budgets and Stock Options (in a growing, high value multiplier market) available to all engineering staff.

 

The VP of Engineering is ex Microsoft, AOL and Yahoo and sets the bar very high, you’ll be working with top class technical talent who enjoy mentoring and knowledge sharing.

 

Our Bangalore office is based in Helios Business Park, close to Walmart Labs on the Outer Ring Road, Kadubeesanahalli, Varthur Hobli, 560103

 

A range of roles and varied technologies are used within Jumio. Crucially, we are always looking at improving our products with new technologies and welcome proofs of concept and new approaches. Graduate Engineers are exposed to all areas of the engineering and product life-cycle before specializing. At a high-level, roles and skills include:

 

Back-End Software Engineer – Java 8, Spring (Core/Boot/Security/MVC/Data) REST Principles, SQL (performant queries against 100M+ tables), Git, Maven / Gradle, JPA, Design Patterns

 

Software Development Engineer in Test – E2E automation testing of Spring based web applications, Java / Groovy / Python, Spring Boot, Docker, Selenium, AWS

 

DevOps Engineer – AWS, infrastructure design, operating, scaling, monitoring and administering production web services at scale. Use of Infrastructure as Code, ideally Terraform, Kubernetes.

Package Details : All engineering staff at Jumio receive additional benefits which are reviewed and improved periodically. These currently include health insurance for the individual, their spouse, 2 children and parents with cover up to 3L. All engineering hires also qualify for Stock Options as part of the package. Additionally, there is a training allowance for each member of staff – this includes: €2000 personal annual budget for professional learning opportunities (training, workshops, conferences etc.) €300 for personal development (not necessarily work related), €500 for team learning and development. €400 for shaping your workplace (adjustable desk, chair etc). Plus additional team budget for moderated team-building events, a team budget for social events (bowling, dinner etc). Jumio staff also benefit from flexible working hours and working remotely (in coordination with the team), social corporate events (sports, celebrations etc) – but above all, a working environment where you can really make a difference, take decisions and be constantly challenged.
