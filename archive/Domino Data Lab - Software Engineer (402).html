Description :

Domino has an ambitious vision for data science. Our platform helps data science teams accelerate
research, increase collaboration, and rapidly deploy predictive models. Our customers are the most
sophisticated analytical organizations in the world, including Monsanto, Allstate, and Instacart. Backed
by Sequoia Capital, Zetta Venture Partners, Bloomberg Beta, and In-Q-Tel, we are at the epicenter of the
data science revolution, helping companies build better cars, develop more effective medicine, or simply
recommend the best song to play next.


Why Start Your Career At Domino?
● Growth - Startups like Domino provide massive opportunity to stretch, there's so much
responsibility that you'll be challenged to grow rapidly, taking broader responsibility than you'd
get at a large tech company
● Revolution - The next era of computing is based in Data Science, Machine Learning, and AI -
Domino is at the forefront of making that transition a reality
● Mentorship - Our engineering team provides great mentorship and learning for junior hires
● Learning - Domino's full stack software engineering role means you'll learn a diverse skillset
including Scala, Kubernetes, React, AWS, and Docker - all highly-sought-after, modern
technologies that will help build your skillset and resume
● Culture - We're not your typical startup focused on lifestyle and perks - we're a professional
team focused on high standards, low ego, great communication, and a growth mindset.
● Challenges - Our software isn't a simple CRUD app, we deal with challenges like job distribution,
cluster management, cybersecurity defense, cloud computing, full stack app development, and
complex integration of a plethora of data science tools and libraries


We have these exciting challenges for you tackle:
● Job distribution
● Cluster management and orchestration
● Asynchronous web development
● Cybersecurity
● Diverse deployments: cloud, bare-metal, and hybrid
● Best-of-breed data science tools and platforms
Our stack is Scala/Java, Play, lots of Docker, MongoDB, and Backbone.js. Ideally we'd like someone who's
comfortable with these technologies, but we're always excited to meet great engineers who can quickly
learn new technologies.
