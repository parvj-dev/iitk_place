 About us:                                                                                                                                                                                             Acuvon Consulting is a management consulting firm with its headquarters in New Delhi, India and representative offices in Riyadh (KSA) and Dubai (UAE).

Our clients belong to diverse set of sectors i.e. Banking, Non-banking financial services (including Investment Banking, Private Equity and Family Offices), Retail, Food & Beverage, Manufacturing, Healthcare and Public Sector (Government).

We have four major service offerings - business accelerator, corporate turnaround, transaction advisory and performance improvement (www.acuvon.com for details)

Our mandates typically range from developing business growth plans with a roadmap and implementation plan, financial management and venture capital support, strategic, operational and financial restructuring, business model validation, development of business cycles, performance reporting, commercial and financial due diligence (Buy side), investor pitch and investor relations support, deal guidance and business closure support (Sell side).

Our employees are graduates of top-tier engineering and business schools and bring in a diverse set of prior experiences. We are an equal opportunity employer

 

 Role Summary:                                                                                                                                                                               As an Analyst, after a brief training you will support one of our client-facing teams. Your role would require you to support the Associates and Project Lead in project delivery. You may be required to work on different work streams involving activities such as market research, financial analysis and modeling, conducting and documenting interviews, analyzing data and developing insights and creating presentations and dashboards. In addition, you may be staffed on internal projects related to organizational development from time to time. This role could require you to travel to client location (outside India) for at-least 3 weeks/month.

 

Expectations:                                                                                                    

  • You should be comfortable to travel to and stay in Saudi Arabia for 3 weeks every month
  • Solve simple to medium complexity client problems and create relevant deliverables
  • Conduct primary/secondary research as may be required
  • Be willing to support and actively contribute to the team as may be required
  • Assist in preparation of business proposals and participate in internal initiatives of the firm

·

 Education, Experience and skill-set:                                                      

  • Excellent problem solving and communication skills (required)
  • Advanced Proficiency in MS Excel and PowerPoint (preferable)
  • Exposure to one or more of: Retail / Banking / Manufacturing (preferable)
  • Exposure to Financial analysis, data analysis, process development / improvement (preferable)

 

We arrange for travel to client location and also ensure comfortable accommodation at the client-site for our employees.
