Description :

Summary

Log 9 Materials is seeking a talented and passionate candidate in mechanical domain. The candidate will be responsible for design and manufacturing, design and fabrication of mechanical components of the products. Also developing research objectives, projects and proposals, undertaking research and evaluating the outcomes. He/she will be responsible for compiling and analyzing and presenting the results to concerned senior management.

 

Responsibilities

  • Design or redesign mechanical devices using analysis and computer-aided design
  • Developing, testing and evaluating theoretical designs
  • Using research, analytical, conceptual and planning skills, particularly mathematical modelling and computer-aided design
  • Evaluate mechanical systems and products by designing and conducting research, applying principles of mechanics, thermodynamics, hydraulics and heat transfer
  • Confirm system capabilities by designing feasibility methods and testing properties
  • Develop manufacturing processes by designing and modifying equipment for fabricating, assembling or installing components
  • Check system and product quality by designing testing methods and confirming the functionality of fabrication, assembly, and installation processes
  • Prepare product reports by collecting, analyzing, and summarizing information and trends

 

Eligibility

Bachelor’s degree or Master’s degree in Mechanical engineering or related field

 

Skills and Experience

  • Industrial or academic internships in related field
  • Knowledge of engineering design and manufacturing tools (e.g., AutoCAD, ANSYS, Matlab)
  • Familiarity with fluid dynamics
  • Experience in fabrication and/or integration
  • Ability to work efficiently with minimal supervision
  • Ability to work independently as well as within a team
