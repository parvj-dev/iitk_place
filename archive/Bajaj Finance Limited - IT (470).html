Position Summary:

The resource hired will be deployed in one of the following roles: Portals/ Application Program Interface (API Development)/ Business Intelligence/ Business Applications (SFDC, Finnone, Pennant etc)/ Payments Technologies/Cloud & Security/ Mobility/ Emerging Technology)
Duties and Responsibilities
A brief description of roles in the above functions is given below:

Portals 
•    Creation, delivery and ongoing management and enhancements of internal/customer facing digital properties, websites and portals.
•    Planning, execution and delivery of all digital properties and channels across multiple devices and resolutions.
•    Work with partners/vendors and internal teams to create websites/portals and apps using multiple    technologies (preferably Open source) such as Share-point, Angular 7, React JS, HTML5, .NET MVC, CSS3, SVG animations.
Application Program Interface (API Development)
•    Understanding of an MVC framework, Java core, HTTP protocol (method/response codes), REST/RESTful web services, SOAP (optional), JSON or XML.
•    Understand the technology infrastructure and Application architecture end to end. 
•    Take part in evaluation of new products, POC, Time bound RND and solution deliveries. 

Business Intelligence
•    Development and update of various management/ Business dashboards using Power BI/Excel/Power point.
•    Development of Tableau/BI dashboards
•    Delivering insights to internal stakeholders related to the business performance and customer data along with the suitable data visualizations while working on multiple data sets which provide information from various market perspectives.
•    Should be able to analyze quantitative trends with secondary research, conducting Market Intelligence studies, in order to have an edge over the competition in the highly saturated market, and data Analysis using Power BI/R.
Business Applications (SFDC, Finnone, Pennant etc)
•    Would be responsible to handle new generation applications using cloud enabled technologies with help of new generation application development processes. 
•    Responsible for Business requirement, Technical design, Business test cases, Training manual document preparation, Change process management and status reporting of SFDC Orgs.
•    Execute and liaison with users for SFDC requirement. 
Payments Technologies
•    Managing Solutioning and Deliveries for Credit Card Business on Salesforce Platform.
•    Must be conversant with coding (JAVA), Linux based OS and databases.
•    Interact with wallet partners integration /engineering team to manage and drive the technical deliveries.
Cloud & Security
•    Opportunity to work on Cloud Computing (Azure, AWS) and Automation with Ansible/Python which cover intensive exposure to Automating critical tasks. 
•    Administrating Security tools implemented in our existing network Scan used for anti-malware protection on endpoints, Network Security, IDS/IPS Monitoring, Firewall and Proxy (Zscalar) Monitoring.
•    Thoroughly looking for latest advisories, threats and vulnerabilities provided by vendors.
Mobility
•    Establish mobile channel as preferred customer/partner & employee engagement channel. 
•    Introduce new and path breaking ideas in the field of mobile through benchmarking with industry best practices.
•    Leads the App from originating the requirement to its delivery i.e.  Need identification, requirement gathering, coordinating with agency/marketing agency for designing of mobile frontend, interacting with internal IT delivery team and QA.
Emerging Technology
•    Implement Consumerized technologies for employees, branch and customers, track usage of the technologies deployed, increase usage using different strategies etc.
•    Monitor to ensure that devices / apps deployed are in good shape and there is adequate usage by customers / partners /employees.
•    Travel to multiple locations to understand challenges of market, local population, branch setup etc if required. 

Basic Qualifications:
•    BE/ B. Tech in any stream 
•    Should demonstrate high analytical, problem solving and critical thinking ability.
•    Ability to think logically and structure problems statements
•    Appreciation for and zeal to solve large business problems through practical solutions
•    Inquisitiveness around technology changes and ability to quickly adapt to these changes
•    Should have numerate bent of mind.
•    Ability to question status-quo and suggest alternate solutions
•    Strong hands-on nature (ability to self-code is a must).
•    Strong IT & Business interactions capability
•    Strong team player with exceptional program management. Self-driven & high ownership level is a key.
