Purpose and Objectives
The Intelligent ERP initiative will make SAP S/4HANA the first AI-enabled ERP solution in the
market, through a broad adoption of AI technologies. It brings together SAP S/4HANA
development, technology development, and customers in a Co-innovation Engagement.
In a flexible and fast manner, the newly founded Rapid Prototyping Team, consisting of fullstack software engineers, architects, and data scientists, comes up with tangible prototypes
and concepts for these high-priority inquiries.
Join us in helping our customers to reimagine their business and create new possibilities that
allow them to dream bigger, work smarter and change their worlds.
Expectations and Tasks
Being a strong member of a technology savvy team, you will be responsible for implementing
cutting-edge business applications that offer a consumer-grade experience in the business
world.
In this role you will conduct and support various projects in artificial intelligence/machine
learning/conversational UI.
You will work closely together with UI/UX designers as well as architects and machine learning
engineers to ensure we offer the best possible user experience. This is an ideal role for a very
focused and ambitious individual who can work in a highly flexible environment.
At SAP we have diversity at heart, so you will also be able to work with the brightest minds
from a multitude of cultural and ethnical backgrounds.
