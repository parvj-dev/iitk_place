Overview of HCCI & Hexagon

Hexagon Capability Center India (HCCI) is one of the largest technology R&D hubs for Hexagon globally. Innovations, consistent deliveries and ever-evolving technologies at HCCI have helped various Hexagon units to grow their business and customer base across the world. HCCI, one of the earliest IT companies in the city of Hyderabad, has a rich history of 31 years (formerly known as Intergraph) and currently employs 1200+ workforce contributing to the core product lines of Hexagon.

Hexagon is a Digital Solution company, delivering solutions that improve productivity and quality across manufacturing, geospatial, infrastructure, safety and mobility applications. With 3.5 B EUR in sales, Hexagon make 24% operating margin, having 18,000 employees across 50 countries.

 

Overview of product

Xalt is a framework, primarily a system of services. Its Digital Capabilities incorporating modern solutions’ architecture provides with AI domain, Edge connectivity and Computing, Cloud computation, Process and Data orchestration, 2D/3D & AR/VR Visualisation, Enterprise integration, Imaging and Video-analytics, Positioning and Navigation among others.
